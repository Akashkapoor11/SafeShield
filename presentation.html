⚠️ Digital Arrest Scams: A National Emergency
Criminals impersonating CBI, ED, and Customs officers trap victims in multi-day video call "digital custody." Average loss per victim: ₹4–15 lakh. Targets: professionals, retirees, homemakers.
⚠️ These Are Industrialised Operations
Run from fraud compounds across borders. AI-generated voices. Spoofed government numbers. Fake portals identical to official sites. One network can run 50+ simultaneous scam calls.
The Problem
The data exists. The intelligence layer to act on it does not.
"What law enforcement lacks is not evidence after the fact — it is intelligence before mass victimisation occurs, and reliable tools to detect threats at the point of contact rather than the point of complaint." — Problem Statement 6
🔴
Reactive, Not Predictive
Victims file complaints only after losing money. Average time from crime to report: 3–7 days. Money has moved across 5+ accounts by then.
🔴
Siloed Intelligence
Banks, telecom, and police data live in separate systems. No unified view means the same criminal ring operates across multiple jurisdictions undetected.
🔴
No Citizen Tool
Citizens have no reliable, multilingual tool to verify if they are being scammed in real time. By the time they search "is this a scam," they've already transferred money.
💡
SafeShield AI solves all three simultaneously — a unified intelligence layer that fuses signals from telecom, banking, and citizen channels into a real-time PreCrime risk picture, and acts on it before victimisation, not after.
