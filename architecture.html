Evaluation Criteria — How SafeShield Wins
💡
Innovation (25%)
PreCrime Score — composite predictive risk before crime occurs. Multi-modal fusion of voice, transaction, graph, and image signals in a single real-time pipeline. India-first multilingual citizen AI.
● Unique approach to proactive prevention
📈
Business Impact (25%)
₹1,776 Cr lost to digital arrest scams in 9 months (MHA). 1.14M cybercrime complaints in 2023, up 60%. 10% interception rate = ₹177 Cr saved annually. ROI positive in Year 1.
● Quantified, massive national impact
⚙️
Technical Excellence (20%)
5 specialized AI agents, Graph Neural Networks, LLM-powered citizen interface, on-device CV model for currency, Kafka + Flink streaming, blockchain evidence packaging.
● Production-grade architecture
🚀
Scalability (15%)
Microservices on Kubernetes. Horizontal scaling for each agent independently. Multi-tenant for 28 state police forces. Federated learning — banks contribute without sharing raw data.
● National-scale from Day 1
🎨
User Experience (15%)
Law enforcement dashboard: dark command-center UI, live alerts, interactive network graph. Citizens: WhatsApp / IVR / Web in 12 languages. Real-time call simulation demo for judges.
● Demo-first, judge-ready
⚖️
Legal Admissibility
All evidence packages blockchain-timestamped and structured per IT Act 2000 Sec. 65B. Audit trail for every AI decision. Chain of custody maintained from detection to court submission.
● Court-ready evidence automation
🌏
Deployment Path
Phase 1: MHA pilot — 5 metros, 6 months. Phase 2: 10 states with high cybercrime rates. Phase 3: National rollout with all 28 states + UTs. Integrates with existing NCRB portal.
● Clear 18-month roadmap
🔐
Data Privacy
Federated learning preserves bank data sovereignty. PII anonymization at ingestion. DPDP Act 2023 compliant. Zero raw personal data stored beyond 30 days without legal hold.
● Privacy-by-design
